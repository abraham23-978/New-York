New York City

New York City (NYC) is the
most populous city in the
United States, with a 2025
estimated population of 8,584,629.
Known as "The Big Apple,"
it serves as a global hub for
finance, culture, and media,
housing the world's two largest
stock exchanges (NYSE and
Nasdaq). The city is famously
divided into five distinct boroughs,
each with its own character

New York City Boroughs!

  • The Bronx.
  • Brooklyn.
  • Queens.
  • Staten Island.
  • Manhattan.

Key Facts for Visitors!

  • Major Landmarks:
    You can explore world-class sites such as
    Times Square, the Statue of
    Liberty, and The Metropolitan Museum of Art.
  • Getting Around:
    NYC is a premier walking city with a 24/7 subway system,
    buses, and the iconic yellow taxis.
  • Current Status:
    As of April 2026, public services like
    schools and trash collection are on
    schedule, and alternate side parking remains in effect.
  • Cultural Scene:
    The city continues to host major events
    at venues like New York City Center, including upcoming
    performances by Ballet Hispánico
    and the Paris Opera Ballet in mid-2026.
